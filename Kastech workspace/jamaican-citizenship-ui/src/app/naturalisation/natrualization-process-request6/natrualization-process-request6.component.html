APPLICATION BY AN ALIEN OR BRITISH PROTECTED PERSON
FOR A CERTIFICATE OF NATURALISATION

To complete the application process the following documents must be scanned and uploaded :

  • The applicant 's Birth Certificate;
  • The applicant's father's or mother's Birth Certficate or Certificate of Jamaica Citizenship or Adoption Certificate issued by the Registrar General Dept.
  • Two (2) passport sized pictures of the applicant certified by either a Justice of the Peace, Commissioner of Oaths, Notary Public , Lawyer or Doctor.

    (The applicant's name along with the name, number and signature of the certifying officer and the certfication date no older of than 6 months should be included).

  • Marriage Certificate (if applicable);
  • Applicant's valid ID ( if adult 18 years and olders);
  • Jamaican parent's government issued ID ( Valid Passport or Driver's Licence), if applicant is a minor;
  • Court issued document evidencing Legal Guardianship where applicable;
  • Official Change of Name Document ( Court Order, Deed Polls registered through the court or the country's records office, etc) where applicable.

All documents must be originals. PICA reserves the right to request additional and/or updated information during the process of the application
